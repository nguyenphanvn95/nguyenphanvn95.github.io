Tinh nang chinh

Goi y ngay tren ban co

Doc FEN truc tiep tu xiangqi.com, phan tich voi engine va ve mui ten ngay tren ban co dang choi.

Panel gon hon

Khi chua phat hien ban co, panel chi con header. Khi co ban co, panel tu mo rong lai nhu ban Chess Killer.

Cai dat noi bo

Khong can popup setting rieng. Moi tuy chon depth, so dong, mau mui ten, auto move va backup deu nam ngay trong panel.

Cach cai dat

1. Cai Tampermonkey

Neu trinh duyet chua co userscript manager, hay cai Tampermonkey truoc.

2. Cai CCA

Mo link cai dat ben duoi va chap nhan install trong Tampermonkey.

https://raw.githubusercontent.com/nguyenphanvn95/nguyenphanvn95.github.io/main/cca/cca.user.js

Cach su dung

  1. Mo trang xiangqi.com va vao mot van co.
  2. CCA se tu detect ban co. Khi board chua san sang, panel chi hien header de gon man hinh.
  3. Nhấn nut banh rang de mo cai dat ngay trong panel.
  4. Chinh depth, so nuoc goi y, kieu mui ten, delay auto move va phim tat theo nhu cau.
  5. Neu can xem lai the co, mo analysis board.
