Cai Dat

De su dung day du, ban nen cai ca userscript va extension quan ly userscript. Neu ban da co Tampermonkey hoac Violentmonkey, chi can them file script.

1

Cai extension quan ly userscript

Cai Tampermonkey hoac Violentmonkey tren Chrome, Edge, Brave hay cac trinh duyet Chromium. Day la phan "ext" de script co the chay tren chess.com va lichess.

2

Cai file userscript

Bam vao link chesskiller.user.js. Extension se mo man hinh xac nhan, sau do chon install.

3

Mo chess.com hoac lichess

Tai trang choi co, panel Chess Killer se hien o goc duoi phai. Neu chua thay, thu reload trang sau khi cai script.

4

Chinh Settings trong panel

Bam nut banh rang trong header panel de mo tab cai dat noi bo. Khong can vao trang setting.html nua.

Goi y: Neu browser chan popup hoac script chua cap nhat, hay save lai userscript trong Tampermonkey va hard reload trang co.

Huong Dan Su Dung

Sau khi cai xong, day la cac thao tac co ban de dung panel hieu qua.

1

Theo doi hint dung phe

Script chi phan tich khi den luot cua phe nguoi choi. Luc doi thu di, panel se chuyen ve trang thai cho va an hint cu.

2

Mo tab cai dat

Bam nut banh rang trong header panel de doi depth, line, ELO limit, auto move, delay, mau mui ten va import/export JSON.

3

Review sau khi ket thuc van

Tren chess.com, nut Review this game trong modal cuoi van se tu dong fetch PGN va mo trang review phu hop.

4

Su dung nut Home va Analysis

Nut Home tren header panel mo trang /cca/index.html. Nut con lai mo analysis.html de xem cong cu phan tich rieng.

FAQ Nhanh

Khong thay panel?

Kiem tra userscript da enable chua, sau do reload lai trang dang choi co.

Bam settings ma khong hien?

Ban can cap nhat ban script moi nhat. Settings hien nay nam ngay trong panel, khong mo trang rieng.

Review khong lay duoc PGN?

Thu lai sau vai giay neu game vua ket thuc, vi archive/callback cua chess.com co the cap nhat cham.

Co the export cau hinh khong?

Co. Tab Settings trong panel da co nut xuat va nhap JSON.
