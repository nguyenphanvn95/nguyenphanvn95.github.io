Mo trang choi co

FAQ Nhanh

Chi hoat dong khi den luot ban, da co engine hints va panel dang lay dung FEN/turn tu board.

Review khong fetch duoc PGN?

Thu lai sau vai giay neu game vua ket thuc, vi callback hoac archive cua chess.com co the cap nhat cham.

Settings o dau?

Nam ngay trong panel, mo bang nut banh rang. Khong can mo setting.html.

Co can app rieng khong?

Khong. Chi can browser, extension quan ly userscript va file chesskiller.user.js.
